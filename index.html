BIO!

Shahrukh Khan was born on 2nd November 1965,also known by the initialism SRK. is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality who works in Hindi films. Referred to in the media as the "Baadshah of Bollywood" he has appeared in more than 80 films, and earned numerous accolades, including 14 Filmfare Awards.
